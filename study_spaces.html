Library Study Areas

Each library on campus provides designated areas for quiet study and collaboration. Popular libraries include the Hatcher Graduate Library and the Shapiro Undergraduate Library.

  • Hatcher Graduate Library: 8 AM - 2 AM
  • Shapiro Undergraduate Library: 24/7 during the academic year

Study Lounges

Many buildings, including student unions and academic buildings, have lounges perfect for group study or individual work.

  • Michigan Union Study Lounge
  • Pierpont Commons Study Lounge
