Food Pantry Resources

The University of Michigan offers a variety of resources through its food pantry to help students facing food insecurity and ensuring access to nutritious meals.

Location and Hours

The food pantry is located at [insert location]. It is open from Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM.

Eligibility and Access

All students enrolled at the University of Michigan are eligible to use the food pantry services. To access these resources, students must present their student ID.

Types of Items Available

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Fresh produce
  • Personal care items

How to Volunteer or Donate

Students and community members are encouraged to volunteer or donate to support the food pantry. For more information on volunteering or donation opportunities, contact foodpantry@umich.edu.
