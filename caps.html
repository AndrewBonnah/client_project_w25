Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

CAPS provides mental health services and support to students at the University of Michigan, fostering personal well-being and academic success.

Urgent Support

If you are in need of immediate assistance, please visit the Urgent Support page on the CAPS website for resources and guidance.

Services Offered

  • Individual Counseling
  • Group Therapy
  • Workshops and Outreach Programs
  • Urgent Support and Crisis Intervention

How to Access Services

Students can access CAPS services by scheduling an appointment through the CAPS website or by calling the office directly at (734) 764-8312. Walk-in hours are also available for urgent needs.

Location and Hours

CAPS is located at the Michigan Union, within easy reach for students on the central campus. Regular operating hours are 8 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday.

Resources and Self-Help Tools

CAPS offers a range of self-help tools and resources on their website, including meditation guides, stress management techniques, and links to external mental health resources.
